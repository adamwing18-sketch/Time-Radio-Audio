# Terry Performance Guide (EVT-V2A)

Voice engine and identity kept constant across all versions:
- Engine: Microsoft Edge Neural TTS
- Voice: en-US-EmmaMultilingualNeural

## Version A - Natural conversation
- What changed: rate -6%, pitch +0Hz, neutral sentence rhythm.
- Why it was changed: create an everyday, person-to-person baseline voice.

## Version B - Morning radio host
- What changed: rate -2%, pitch +2Hz, slightly brighter delivery.
- Why it was changed: add light morning energy while staying warm and natural.

## Version C - Gentle companion
- What changed: rate -10%, pitch -1Hz, softer pacing and calmer phrasing.
- Why it was changed: strengthen reassurance and companion feeling.

## Version D - Bedtime companion
- What changed: rate -14%, pitch -3Hz, slower rhythm and lower tone.
- Why it was changed: produce a restful, end-of-day listening style.
